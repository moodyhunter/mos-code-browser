13 * \brief Flags for ipc_channel_open()
14 * \details
15 * FLAG & EXISTENCE | EXISTING | NON-EXISTING
16 * -----------------+-----------+--------------
17 * CREATE_ONLY | FAIL | CREATE
18 * EXISTING_ONLY | OPEN | FAIL
19 * neither | OPEN | CREATE
20 * both (invalid) | FAIL | FAIL
21 */
